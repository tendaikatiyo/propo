Help & Methodology

Understand how the dashboard calculates market signals and what each score means.

Yield Calculation

Yield is calculated using median rental income and median sale price for the selected market. It is shown as gross yield and helps compare cash flow potential across suburbs.

Confidence Score

Confidence is based on the number of rental and sale listings available in the market. Higher scores indicate more data support for the metrics.

Opportunity Score

Opportunity combines yield, listing volume, and market balance to highlight suburbs that may be attractive for investment.

City Selection

Results in the Investment Finder are limited to the selected city. Only Harare and Bulawayo are currently available to ensure comparison based on strong market coverage.
